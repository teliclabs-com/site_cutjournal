# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 5, 2026**

CutJournal AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the CutJournal AI iOS application.

## We Do Not Collect Your Personal Data

CutJournal AI does **not** collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to our servers. All of your data — including body measurements, progress photos, goals, and health data — is stored **exclusively on your device**.

**Photos are never stored on our servers.** When you use the AI body fat estimation feature, your photo is sent for analysis and immediately discarded after processing. No photos are retained, saved, or stored on any server at any time.

## What We Do Store

The only piece of information we store on our servers is a **unique device identifier**. This ID is used solely to validate your device for access to the AI estimation service. It is not linked to any personal information.

## Apple Health Integration

CutJournal AI can read and write health data to Apple Health with your explicit permission. This data stays on your device and is managed entirely through iOS. We do not have access to your Apple Health data.

## Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or share any user information with third parties. We have no user data to share.

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all your on-device data at any time by removing the app
- Revoke Apple Health permissions through iOS Settings

## Children's Privacy

CutJournal AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
